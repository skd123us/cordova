OfficeClip

OfficeClip Mobile provides smartphone access to its suite of business software. OfficeClip is used by thousands of companies to manage contacts, customers, projects, timesheets, expenses, support issues and invoices.

It integrates with Google Contacts, Google Calendar, Outlook, QuickBooks, Sage 50, ADP and other systems.

It is available in two editions:

  1. Basic - Free for 10 users
  2. Enterprise - Has all the advanced features. See the feature page for details.
